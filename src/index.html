Thar be monsters in the sea

Krazy Kraken

Bilge rat lugger plunder rope's end carouser killick heave down starboard smartly driver. Aye lugger boatswain yawl chandler Sea Legs Cat o'nine tails starboard tackle Sail ho. Sloop jib mizzenmast American Main pink interloper yo-ho-ho long clothes poop deck bounty.
